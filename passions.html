My Passions


Dance


I have been an Indian classical and fusion dancer since the age of three. I have done many stage performances and competitions throughout the years. It was a great way for me to connect with my Indian roots and quickly became a favorite hobby of mine. To this day I continue to dance at community events or through school.

I learned traditional style dance called Andhra Natyam and Bollywood fusion from family and family friends. I got to perform on many stages and even teach younger kids dances. Once I got to college, I joined a co-ed Bollywood fusion competitive dance team. We got to travel around the country and compete against other college teams. It has always been a passion of mine - it taught me a lot of discipline and the reward behind hard work.

I started performing traditional Indian Dance at the age of three.


I have performed on stage for over 15 years though community events and competitions.
